Welcome to YeHui Lab

Our research focuses on developing and implementing an array of novel analytical tools in the area of metabolomics and chemical proteomics to elucidate the direct binding targets of small molecules including natural medicines and endogenous metabolites, and gain new insights into how these functional molecules regulate protein function and signaling network.
Ye group is under the Jiangsu Provincial Key Laboratory of Pharmacokinetics led by Prof. Guangji Wang, the academician of Chinese Academy of Engineering majoring in Pharmacokinetics, and Prof. Haiping Hao, the Yangtse River Scholar and the recipient of the National Science Fund for Distinguished Young Scholars. The super-lab is equipped with state-of-the-art mass spectrometer facilities for target discovery and molecular biology tools for target validation.
